photography ★

Windows of Bombay is a visual and storytelling project that explores the hidden emotions, histories, and everyday lives behind the city’s windows. What started as a study of architecture grew into a deeper reflection on memory, change, and identity in Bombay. Through photography and conversations with residents, the project captures how windows act as silent witnesses to a fast-moving, ever-changing city. Each window tells a story—some of pride, some of loss, some of hope. This project aims to slow down the gaze, encouraging viewers to see Bombay not just as a city, but as a living memory.
